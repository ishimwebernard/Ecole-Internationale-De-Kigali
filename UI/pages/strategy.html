Duties of Students

Students should understand the role they must play in their own success. Students are the backbone of a good academic success because all other factors are complete. Parents can pay school fees (tuition fees) and teachers provide lessons (or instill knowledge) but without the will of the student with a good academic achievement can not be held Do all homework assigned is one of the best effective ways and the best way to get good results at school. Even if the duties are not listed for the promotion of class, however it is a profitable exercise for the advancement of knowledge. This will allow students to work well in tests and examinations. This practice instills in students the notion that the rules at school. Regularly attend lessons: Students have always in mind that they should participate in the lessons permanently. They should have thirst to follow and participate regularly in lessons, even if they are a little sick, they will endeavor to come to school. What students are asked to : The study: Students should be used for personal study. Remind them that they came to study. They must be responsible.
